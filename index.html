ABOUT US

Welcome to the world of Mira Essence, where passion meets perfection in every layer, fold, and swirl.
Our pastries are more than just sweet treats, they’re moments of pure indulgence crafted with heart and heritage. pastry isn’t just food, it’s an expression of art, passion, and purpose. Founded with a vision to blend tradition and innovation, our brand stands for authenticity, creativity, and the pure joy that comes from every bite.
